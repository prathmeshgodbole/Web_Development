Mobirise
Remember that Mobirise does not save the projects into the cloud, you need to export your site and then import it on another device.
To do that, follow this instruction.
To Export.
1) Click on the hamburger.
2) Click on the "Sites".
3) Choose the site you want to export.
4) Click on blue gear and "Export Site".
5) Now you can choose any folder you want and put there your site.
To import.
1) Click on hamburger.
2) Click on "Sites".
3) Click on the "Import Site".
